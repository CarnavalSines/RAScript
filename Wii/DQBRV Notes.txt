## Game Modes

### 1. Arcade Mode (アーケードモード)
Two sub-modes:
- **Solo (ひとりであそぶ):** 2 battles (1st round + finals). Win well enough → Demon Lord fight; Demon Lord can lead to Great Demon Lord appearance.
- **2-Player Co-op (ふたりであそぶ):** Starts directly at Demon Lord fight. Win → Great Demon Lord guaranteed.
- **Demon Lord unlock:** Starts with Dragonlord (竜王) only. Additional Demon Lords / Great Demon Lords unlock as you win tournaments in Tournament Mode.
- No rival intrusions in Arcade Mode (unlike arcade version).
- Defeating a Great Demon Lord awards a specific SP Card; a mid-run defeat awards a Monster Card.

### 2. Tournament Mode (大会モード)
The Wii-exclusive story mode. Set in the near-future Japanese town of **Ryujin-cho (竜神町)**. Player creates an avatar and progresses through a tournament bracket structure.

### 3. Versus Battle (対戦バトルロード)
- **Local (おうちで対戦):** 1v1, requires 2 controllers.
- **Wi-Fi (Wi-Fiで対戦):** National matchmaking + friend code battles. BP (Battle Points) tracked on national leaderboard.

### 4. Legend Quest (レジェンドクエスト) — PAID DLC
- Paid downloadable content. Recreates story scenarios from mainline DQ games.
- Confirmed: **Legend Quest VIII** (DQ8 scenario — defeat Dhoulmagus, Rhapthorne, then Dragon God King)
- Additional Legend Quests purchasable via Wii Points (Nintendo Points).
- Legend Mode was free in the arcade version; on Wii it became paid DLC.
- Legend Quest also has SP cards exclusive to it (e.g., Gigaslash, Super High Tension, etc.).

### 5. Tutorial (遊び方をおぼえる)
Single training battle with Morrie narration. Awards XP normally.

## Tournament Mode — Full Progression

### Town: Ryujin-cho (竜神町)
Key locations:
- **Home** — Change costumes, watch past SP/finisher movies
- **Card Shop Morita** — Buy cards with Gold or Small Medals; also has a card gacha machine (100G/pull). Post-game: Morrie Mission unlocks here.
- **Costume Shop** — Buy costumes (おしゃれ着)
- **Game Center** — Chain battles vs. NPCs; more wins = more cards/gold rewarded, but a loss ends the run with no reward and no team change allowed mid-run
- **Toudoukan (闘道館)** — Dojo run by Shishō. Tutorial + job advancement battles (転職チャレンジバトル)
- **Colosseum Center (コロシアムセンター)** — Tournament venue
- **Torneko's Fountain (トルネコの噴水)** — Throw gold coins to gain Merchant job XP; eventually yields the **Legendary Merchant (伝説の商人)** Legend Card

| Name | Role |

| Shishō (シショー) | Dojo master; Ryuta's father |

### Progression Flow

#### Phase 1 — Tutorial / Introduction
- Lose to Ryuta (scripted loss; he gives you a card — Iron Claws, enabling Martial Artist job)
- Win vs. Hina → reward: **Dracky Outfit (ドラキーのふく)** costume
- Day 2: Town opens up; head to Colosseum

#### Phase 2 — Freshman Tournament (フレッシュマン大会)
- Uses **basic jobs** (基本職) only
- Three confirmed tournaments (by job class); full count needs verification:
  - **Greatest Warrior (最強戦士決定戦)** — 4 rounds: Kohei → Shiori → Patcho → **Daiki** (Lv25)
  - **Greatest Mage (最強魔法使い決定戦)** — 4 rounds: Erika → Yusuke → Shusha → **Tsubasa** (Lv25)
  - **Greatest Martial Artist (最強武闘家決定戦)** — 4 rounds: Yuna → Taichi → Kazu → **Ryuta** (Lv75)
  - *(Likely also Priest, Merchant, Monster Tamer variants — needs in-game confirmation)*
- Win **2 tournaments** → unlocks Master Class

#### Phase 3 — Master Class Tournament (マスタークラス大会)
- Uses **advanced jobs** (上級職) only
- Must first unlock advanced jobs via the Toudoukan (see Job System)
- Win **at least 1 Master Class tournament** → unlocks Representative Hero Battle
- *(Full tournament roster for Master Class needs confirmation)*

#### Phase 4 — Representative Hero Battle (代表勇者決定戦)
- Any job allowed
- 4 rounds: Takumi → Hina → Tsubasa → Daiki
- Win → unlocks Champion Battle + unlocks **Hero (勇者)** job challenge at Toudoukan

#### Phase 5 — Champion Battle (王者決定戦)
- Round 1: Ryuta
- Mid-match: Pisaro intrudes
- Boss fight: Sword Master Pisaro (魔剣士ピサロ)
- Final fight: Death Pisaro (デスピサロ)
- Win → **Ending / Credits (first)**

#### Phase 6 — Post-game: Universe #1 Battle (宇宙一決定戦)
- Unlocks at Colosseum after first ending
- **9 rounds** vs. historical Great Demon Lords (excluding Death Pisaro)
- **10 rounds** vs. "True Great Demon Lords" (真大魔王)
- Boss: **Dark Dreaum (ダークドレアム)**
- Final boss: **True Dark Dreaum (真ダークドレアム)**
- Win → **Second Ending / Second Staff Roll**
- Card machine cap lifted from 5/day to unlimited

#### Phase 7 — Post-game Exhibition & Collectibles
After second ending, the following unlock around town:
- **Hero Kaito** → battle vs. Legendary Hero, Ryan, Barbara → win each = Legend Hero Card
- **Queen Pisaro** (vacant lot) → battle vs. historical Demon Lords → win = Demon Lord Legend Cards (Dragonlord human form first, then others)
- **Pisaro at port** → battle vs. historical True Great Demon Lords → win = True Great Demon Lord Legend Cards (True Dragonlord first, then others)
- **Torneko's Fountain** → throw enough gold → **Legendary Merchant (伝説の商人)** Legend Card
- **Small Medals (ちいさなメダル)** → scattered around town map; spendable at Card Shop
- **Morrie Mission** (Card Shop post-game) → see below

## Morrie Mission (モリーミッション)

Unlocks at Card Shop Morita after clearing Universe #1 Battle. Series of 10 challenge battles with specific win conditions, each awarding a **Roto Collection (ロトコレクション)** SP Card. A final battle vs. Morrie himself rewards the title **"Morrie's Successor (モリーの後継者)"** and the Legend Card **Legendary Monster Tamer (伝説の魔物使い)**.

| # | Mission Name | Condition | Reward |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Fierce Battle! 3-Turn Showdown | Win in ≤3 turns (no Legend cards) | Super High Tension (Roto) |
| 2 | Dodge It! Evasion Mastery | Dodge ≥2 attacks, then win (no Legend cards) | Wind Mantle (Roto) |
| 3 | Perfect Victory! | Win with ≥500 HP remaining (preset equipment/monsters) | Great Defense (Roto) |
| 4 | No SP! Pure Battle | Win in ≤2 turns (no Legend cards, no SP cards) | Falcon Sword (Roto) |
| 5 | Sleep! Lullaby Strategy | Win using sleep (no Legend cards) | Seductive Gaze (Roto) |
| 6 | Easy Win? Finish with Mera | Finish with Mera spell | Water Feathercoat (Roto) |
| 7 | Aim for It! Critical Hit | Land a critical hit to win | Samba de Amigo (Roto) |
| 8 | Can't Move! Cursed Battle | Win while cursed (no Legend cards) | Starfall Bracelet (Roto) |
| 9 | Challenge Legend! Hero Battle | (vs. legendary hero) | Roto's Crest (Roto) |
| 10 | Revival Demon! Final Battle | (final Morrie Mission) | Gigaslash (Roto) |
| +1 | Battle vs. Morrie | Win vs. Morrie | Title + Legendary Monster Tamer card |

## Job System (職業)

### Basic Jobs (基本職)
| Job | Notes |
|-----|-------|
| Warrior (戦士) | Standard physical fighter |
| Mage (魔法使い) | Spell-focused |
| Martial Artist (武闘家) | Speed/claw builds |
| Priest (僧侶) | Healing/support |
| Merchant (商人) | XP gained only via gold thrown into Torneko's Fountain — NOT from battles |
| Monster Tamer (魔物使い) | Monster-focused synergies |

### Advanced Jobs (上級職)
Unlocked at Toudoukan by meeting level requirements. Requirement: basic job at sufficient level (exact thresholds need verification).
| Job | Promoted From |
|-----|---|
| Battle Master (バトルマスター) | Warrior or Martial Artist path |
| Sage (賢者) | Mage or Priest path |
| Monster Master (モンスターマスター) | Monster Tamer path |

### Special Job
| Job | Unlock Condition |
|-----|---|
| Hero (勇者) | Win Representative Hero Battle AND have 2 advanced jobs at Lv50+ |

### Job Levels & Titles
Each job has titles at Lv10, 20, 30, 40, 50, 60, 70, 80, 90, and max level (cap appears to be 99 or 100 — needs confirmation). Max level title is unique per job (e.g., "Battle Road" for Battle Master, "God Mage" for Sage, etc.).

Example — Warrior title progression: Rookie → One-Man Army → Veteran → Expert → Master → Legend Fighter → Seasoned Warrior → Immortal Warrior → Edge of Warrior → Legend Warrior → Eden's Warrior (max)

## Card System

### Card Types
| Type | Notes |
|------|-------|
| Monster Cards | Two pools: Battle Road I roster and Battle Road II roster |
| Weapon Cards (武器) | Equip to hero avatar |
| Shield Cards (盾) | Equip to hero avatar |
| Body Cards (体) | Equip to hero avatar |
| SP Cards (スペシャルカード) | Special abilities used mid-battle; pre-assigned to D-pad or selected mid-battle |
| Legend Cards (レジェンド) | Sub-types: Legend Hero, Legend Demon Lord, Legend Great Demon Lord |
| Fusion Weapon Cards (融合武器) | Created from combining weapon cards |

### Combination Systems
| System | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Type Affinity (タイプ相性) | Bonuses based on monster type combinations |
| 3rd Skill (第3のワザ) | Unlocked when specific monster + job combo is met |
| Habitat Affinity (生息地相性) | Bonuses based on monster habitat combinations |
| Fusion (合体) | Specific monster pairs create a fused form |
| Job Affinity (職業相性) | Bonuses based on avatar job + monster combinations |
| Finisher (必殺技) | Special finishing move triggered by conditions |
| Ultimate Finisher (究極必殺技) | Enhanced finisher, harder to trigger |
| Other Affinity (その他相性) | Miscellaneous bonus combinations |

### Card Sources
- **Battle rewards** (card drops at fight end)
- **Card Shop Morita** (gold or Small Medals)
- **Gacha machine** outside shop (100G per pull)
- **Tournament/Mission rewards** (fixed drops)
- **Wi-Fi DLC Shop** (paid, via Wii Points)
- **Color code scan** (via DSiWare scanner or mobile app — scanning physical arcade cards)

### Notable Card Subsets
- **Roto Collection (ロトコレクション):** SP card variants earned exclusively through Morrie Mission
- **Legend Hero Cards:** Earned from post-game exhibition wins (Kaito side)
- **Legend Demon Lord Cards:** Earned from Queen Pisaro fights
- **Legend Great Demon Lord Cards:** Earned from port Pisaro fights
- **Legendary Merchant:** Earned from Torneko's Fountain

## Kocho's Quiz (コーチョーのクイズ)

School principal gives trivia quizzes about the game/town. Luida at the café provides hints. Likely yields rewards. Full question list at dq-gh.com/brv/quiz.html — not yet researched.



### Data Flow (confirmed)
1. **MEM2** — Character/team definitions written here at equipment selection screen. Stable base; holds calculated max HP and stat totals for the current loadout.
2. **MEM1 `0x017914DC`** Live battle engine working copy. Populated from MEM2 when a battle loads. May be pointer-backed or mode-dependent > address was consistent within a session but not yet confirmed stable across cold boots.
3. **MEM1 `0x01791220+` struct** Per-round snapshot. Copied from `0x017914DC` at the start of each round. Contains current and round-start HP for both player and enemy.

> **Note:** The MEM1 battle struct address held across multiple battles in the same session. Whether it shifts between boots or across different modes (Arcade vs. Tournament) still needs confirmation. MEM2 base is the likely pointer anchor if it does shift.

### Confirmed Battle Struct (`0x01791220+`)

| RA Address | Size | Meaning | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0x0179122C | 16-bit | Player HP at round start | Snapshot taken at round begin |
| 0x01791230 | 16-bit | Player current HP | Updates before on-screen animation |
| 0x01791234 | 16-bit | Enemy HP at round start | Snapshot taken at round begin |
| 0x01791238 | 16-bit | Enemy current HP | Updates before on-screen animation |

> **Timing note:** Both current HP values update at the start of enemy animation, before the on-screen display reflects the change. This means values are settled and stable for achievement logic > no mid-animation flicker

### Other Confirmed Addresses

| RA Address | Meaning | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 0x017914DC | Player current HP (live engine copy) | Source for the struct above; possibly pointer-backed |
| 0x1009d5c0 | Player HP from team selection (MEM2) | Written at equipment screen; stable across battles |
| 0x00830734 | Gold | From Gecko code analysis |
| 0x00830738 | Small Medals | From Gecko code analysis |
